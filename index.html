Welcome to the Realm of Zasha: Where Creativity Meets Consciousness Greetings, I'm Zahid Shaikh, but those who know me in the realms of light call me Zasha. As an original lightworker, I've been weaving my threads of creativity, healing, and technology for years, crafting a unique blend of art, design, and innovation. My essence is that of an Indigo personality – a visionary, a free thinker, and a passionate soul dedicated to elevating human consciousness. The Multifaceted Artisan My expertise spans various disciplines, including motion graphics, visual effects supervision, web design and development, video production, and virtual reality experiences for education and entertainment. I've had the privilege of working with diverse industries, from corporate giants to film productions, NGOs, and beyond. Each project has been an opportunity to hone my skills, expand my knowledge, and channel my creativity. As a self-taught artist, I've relied on my intuition and hands-on experience to master my craft. The creative arts are not just a profession but a passion that flows through my veins like lifeblood. My journey has been marked by numerous challenges, but I've consistently approached them with a wise and resilient spirit. The Lightworker’s Path In 2017, I took the MENSA IQ Test out of curiosity, scoring an impressive 93.7 percentile. This experience not only showcased my analytical abilities but also reinforced my understanding of the intricate connections between creativity, logic, and consciousness. My spiritual journey deepened in 2018 when I began volunteering for the 80G certified NGO, Holistic Training and Development Centre (HTDC). Founded by the visionary Ms. Namrata Kandoi, HTDC's mission resonated deeply with my own values. Under her guidance, I learned the ancient art of Reiki Healing, further expanding my capacity to support and uplift others. Sharing Knowledge and Experiences To disseminate my insights and experiences, I've created a blog – a platform where I share my perspectives on creativity, technology, spirituality, and personal growth. I invite you to explore my writings and join me on this journey of self-discovery and evolution. Your Digital Challenges, My Expertise If you're facing digital dilemmas or seeking innovative solutions, I'm here to help. With my diverse skill set and lightworker’s approach, I'm committed to not only resolving technical issues but also elevating your projects with a deep understanding of human consciousness and creative potential. In the realm of Zasha, creativity, technology, and spirituality converge. Let's collaborate, innovate, and co-create a brighter, more enlightened future together.
